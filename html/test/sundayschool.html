Sunday School

The Proverbs of Solomon King of Israel

Sunday school meets in the Rec Center of Founders Baptist Church at 9am. We begin with a short time of fellowship, followed by songs of worship, a lesson from Scripture, and corporate prayer. Our goal is to start the Lord's Day by praising God, having fellowship with one another, and having our hearts and minds prepared to hear the sermon directly after.
