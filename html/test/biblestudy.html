Weekly Bible Study

We meet for Bible study every Thursday evening, from 7:00 until 9:00. Most of the time is spent digging into a chapter from our book of study. Afterwards we split into small groups for weekly accountability and encouragement. There is an optional prayer meeting prior to Bible Study at 6:30. Food and drinks are provided. Join us as we gather around God's Word to worship our Savior and Lord.
